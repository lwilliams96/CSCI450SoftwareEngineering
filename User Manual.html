Compliplenty User's Manual

1.1 Installing the App

To install the app, download the apk, run it on your device, and it will prompt you to install to your device, click allow app to install.

You may need to grant your device permissions to do this, check your phone's user manual for details on how to do this

1.2 Running the app

To run the app, click the icon on your phone titled "Compliplenty"

A splash screen should appear, and after that, the main screen of the app should appear

1.3 Navigating the app

After the splash screen exits, the main screen should appear

1.4 Accessing contacts

To access the contacts on your device, click the contacts icon in the upper right corner of the main screen

1.5 Edit your profile

To edit your profile, click the person icon in the upper left corner of the app, this will take you to the edit profile screen

1.6 Connecting with Facebook

To connect with facebook, click the icon at the top entitled "Connect with facebook"

This will bring you to your devices default web browser, and ask you to authenticate with Facebook

Confirm the facebook access prompt, and your account is now linked with Facebook

2.1 Using the app

This app works on a questionaire system, the app will ask you a series of questions, and generate a compliment in response to those questions,
To start the question session, press the question button at the bottom of the app

2.2 Sharing to Facebook

To share the compliment to facebook, click the share to Facebook icon, this will open your device's default web browser

Provided you have linked your local account to facebook (see 1.6, Connecting with facebook), a share button should pop up, asking if you wish to share to Facebook, if you respond yes, it will post it and share it with your friends.

2.3 Random compliment generator

If you wish to just have a compliment, without answering the questionaire, you may generate a random compliment by clicking the random button at the bottom of the app.

2.4 Setting a favorite compliment

To set a favortite compliment, go to the profile screen, and press the favorite compliment button.
This will set your compliment of the day as your favorite compliment
